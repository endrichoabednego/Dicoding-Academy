Museum Affandi

Affandi Museum is located at Jalan Laksda Adisucipto number 167 and occupies an area of 3,500 m2 consisting of a museum building consisting of four galleries along with complementary buildings such as a ticket purchase place, two studios, and a house where the painter Affandi and his family live. Affandi himself, the museum also houses paintings by other Indonesian painters, including: Basuki Abdullah, Popo Iskandar, Sudjojono, Hendra Gunawan, Barli Sasmitawinata, and Mochtar Apin. In addition, there are statues by Amrus Natalsya, and polished wood chips by the Tjokot family.

Location : Jl. Laksda B Adi Sucipto 167 55281 Depok

Jogja National Museum

Jogja National Museum (JNM) is a contemporary art museum and gallery which is established under the auspices of the Yayasan Yogyakarta Seni Nusantara (YSSN). JNM provides a special gallery called Gallery for Citizens that can be used by budding artists to hold exhibitions for free. In addition to the showroom, there are also facilities such as the JNM Art Shop, and the Situs Kriya Canteen

Location : Jl. Prof. Ki Amri Yahya no. 1 52281 Yogyakarta Region
